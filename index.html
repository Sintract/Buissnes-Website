About Me

I’m a web designer focused on creating clean, modern, and professional websites for small businesses. My aim is to help businesses build a stronger online presence through websites that not only look good, but also feel trustworthy, easy to use, and tailored to their brand. I understand how important first impressions are online, and I believe every business deserves a website that reflects the quality of the service they provide. I’m particularly interested in working with local businesses and helping them stand out in an increasingly digital world. Whether it’s a tradesperson, a barber, a car detailing business, or another independent service, I enjoy designing websites that make it easier for customers to find information, get in touch, and feel confident in choosing that business. A strong website can make a real difference, and I enjoy being part of that process. My approach to web design is centred around simplicity, clarity, and professionalism. I focus on building websites that are visually appealing, mobile-friendly, and straightforward for visitors to navigate. I pay close attention to layout, spacing, colour, and overall presentation so that each site feels polished and modern while still being practical for the business it represents. As I continue to grow my skills and experience, my goal is to build websites that genuinely add value to businesses and help them present themselves more effectively online. I’m always looking to improve, develop my craft, and create work that is both functional and visually strong.
